Who We Are

I-Crescent Relief Foundation is a non-profit, non-governmental organization based in Nigeria which establishment is based on the realization that active poor has potentials to create jobs and enhancing the quality of life. The Foundation's main objective is to support the active poor with the resources they need to develop their socio- economic activities, business ideas, knowledge and skills more expeditiously for the good of the society. The Foundation is based in Abuja and is founded by people who are concerned about the well- being of less privileged and registered as a corporate non- governmental organization with registration number 157209 on 6th April 2021. Although many NGOs tend to help the deprived and oppressed in society, I-Crescent Relief Foundation is focused on energizing the 'business lights' (active poor no matter their status) in the society with the expectation that they would be self- empowered and contribute to the society by their socio-economic activities. The Foundation is an organization dedicated to providing sustainable solutions to poverty, hunger, and financial inclusion in Nigeria and Africa in general. Through our various program initiatives, we seek to provide assistance to those critically and genuinely in need. With the dedication of our volunteers, staff members, and partners, we strive to improve the lives of those in need within the society. I-Crescent Relief Foundation mobilizes funds and promotes initiatives to help those who need it most. With an array of fund mobilization and volunteer projects, the foundation endeavors to make the world a better place for all. The Foundation is a specialist, impact- driven Small Scale Entrepreneurship financier. We help Small Scale Entrepreneurs succeed by providing them with business financing and support (expert advice and continuous guidance) to grow their businesses. We believe that a growing Small Scale Entrepreneurship sector which creates sustainable jobs is the most powerful driver of social and economic development that truly improves peopleâ€™s lives.

How we Work

I-Crescent Relief Foundation is governed by a Board of Trustee appointed by the founders and its organs are managed on a daily basis by sets of management teams comprising mainly pioneer members and headed by experienced and competent person. There are teams which are the organized structures to enable the foundation functions effectively and efficiently as a body to achieve the mission.

Finance and Fund Mobilization Team

This team manages the finances of the foundation. They are responsible for mobilizing, managing and disbursing funds.

Projects Development Team

This team is responsible for the development and implementation of all our projects.

Business Monitoring and Investment Team

This team monitors and supervises businesses that are being undertaken by our beneficiaries and also be in charge of setting up and managing social projects and business services to generate income for the foundation.

Media and Advocacy Team

This team is devoted to media management and public relations. They are also responsible for running advocacy programs and lobbying decision-makers.

Strategic and Evaluation Team

This team designs the short and long term strategic plans of the foundation and monitors its implementation.

Administration Team

This team is responsible for the general administration of the foundation, its expansion and the provision of logistics to all its arms.
